iSoftStone is Building a Digital Publishing Platform for People’s Education Press(May. 22, 2015)

iSoftStone has been selected by People’s Education Press (PEP), the largest and most authoritative press in China, to establish its Digital Resource Management & Delivery Platform.

PEP is leading the trend of the publishing industry in China, and heading into the digital publishing area as a pioneer. In December 2012, iSoftStone was selected as PEP’s core provider to execute its digital publishing strategy. The Digital Resource Management & Pushing Platform consists of an advanced digital resource pool, as well as an effective content importing system.

iSoftStone has accumulated strong capabilities in digital publishing, and been able to provide end-to-end digital publishing services including digital publication production, online publishing, copyright management and transactions, as well as short-run printing. In the future, the company is expected to work closely with PEP to build its unique advantages in this area.
